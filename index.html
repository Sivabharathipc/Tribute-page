Light Yagami

"Justice will prevail, no matter what."

Light Yagami is the main protagonist of the anime and manga series Death Note. A highly intelligent student, he stumbles upon the Death Note, a notebook that allows him to kill anyone whose name he writes in it. Adopting the identity of "Kira," he seeks to create a utopia free of crime, but his descent into power raises moral and ethical questions.

Key Highlights:

  • Possessed the Death Note, giving him the power to eliminate criminals.
  • Went by the alias "Kira" and was pursued by the world’s greatest detective, L.
  • Used strategic genius to evade capture for years.
  • Became the leader of the NPA Task Force while secretly being Kira.
  • Ultimately met his downfall due to Near's intervention.
